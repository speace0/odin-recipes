Garlic Bread

The most garlicular bread you ever will taste

Do you love bread? Do you love cheese? Do you love garlic? 3 of the silliest questions I've ever asked. Of course you do!

Ingredients

  • 1 bread loaf
  • 100g of butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160C.
  2. Cut the bread horizontally and mix the garlic and salt.
  3. Drizzle with the garlic butter.
  4. Bake for 20 mins until crispy.
  5. Top with mozzarella and bake for another 7 mins.
  6. Enjoy!
