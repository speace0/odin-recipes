Chocolate Cake

The best chocolate cake you will ever make

This chocolate cake is the best you will ever have, and you can make it even as a complete beginner.

Ingredients

  • 200g golden caster sugar
  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g self-rasing flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190C and butter two 20cm round cake tins.
  2. Mix the golden caster sugar, butter, eggs, self-rasing flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, vanilla extract and milk in a large bowl.
  3. Divide the mixture between both tins. Bake for 20 mins.
  4. Leave to cool for 10 mins then decorate.
