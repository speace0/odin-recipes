Pizza

It's pizza time!

Pizza. What more is there to say. Lets get on with it.

Ingredients

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100ml passata
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Mix the flour yeast and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Pour 200ml of warm water and add the olive oil. Bring together until you have a soft, wet dough. Let it rest for up to 24 hours
  3. Mix the passata, basil and garlic together.
  4. Roll out the dough and add the sauce and mozzarella.
  5. Bake for 8-10 mins at 220C.
  6. Enjoy!
